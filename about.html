History Of ENJA Enterprise

ENJA Enterprise is a company operating in the field of architecture and property development, which was founded by Falih Arif Arrazzaq Putra, A.Md.Kom, in early 2024. As part of the ENJA GROUP, this company is committed to presenting high quality innovation and design in every project.

With branches spread across Malang, Yogyakarta and Jakarta, ENJA Enterprise is able to reach various market segments and meet the needs of clients in various regions. Our team consists of architects and experienced professionals who have a vision for creating functional, aesthetic and sustainable spaces.
